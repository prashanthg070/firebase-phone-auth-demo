Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Summary: Firebase Phone Auth Demo uses your phone number solely for authentication purposes. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Firebase Phone Auth Demo ("we," "our," or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using Firebase Phone Auth Demo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Phone Number

We collect and process your phone number for authentication purposes. This is the primary piece of personal information our app requires to function. Your phone number is collected when you:

  • Use the instant carrier-based verification feature
  • Enter your phone number for SMS verification
  • Complete the authentication process

2.2 Authentication Data

We collect authentication tokens and session information generated by Firebase Authentication to maintain your logged-in state and provide secure access to the application.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improving app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and create a secure authenticated session
  • Security: To protect your account from unauthorized access
  • Service Delivery: To provide you with the core functionality of the application
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and resolve technical issues you may encounter
  • App Improvement: To understand how the app is used and improve its features and performance

Important: We do NOT use your phone number or any other data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than authentication and improving the app's core functionality.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Firebase Services

Your phone number is processed through Firebase Authentication and Firebase Phone Number Verification services, which are provided by Google LLC. These services are used solely to verify your phone number and create your authenticated session. Firebase operates under Google's privacy policies and security standards.

4.2 No Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with:

  • Data brokers or information resellers
  • Advertising networks or marketing companies
  • Analytics services for user profiling
  • Any third party for purposes unrelated to authentication

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).

5. How We Store and Protect Your Information

5.1 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Phone numbers are stored securely in Firebase's infrastructure with encryption at rest
  • Access to user data is restricted and logged
  • We follow Firebase and Google Cloud security best practices

5.2 Data Storage Location

Your data is stored on Firebase's secure cloud infrastructure, which is hosted on Google Cloud Platform. Firebase automatically replicates data across multiple geographic regions for reliability and disaster recovery.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Retention Period

We retain your phone number and authentication data for as long as your account remains active. This allows you to use the app without re-authenticating every time.

6.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information at any time. To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the application from your device
  • Contact us at support@example.com to request complete account deletion

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by uninstalling the app

8. Limited Use Disclosure

Firebase Phone Auth Demo's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we only use Google user data (phone numbers obtained via Firebase Phone Number Verification) for:

  • Providing and improving user-facing features of our app
  • Authentication and security purposes

We do NOT use Google user data for:

  • Serving advertisements
  • Creating user profiles for advertising
  • Training AI or machine learning models
  • Any purpose unrelated to authentication

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to Firebase's infrastructure, which complies with applicable data protection regulations.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification for material changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
